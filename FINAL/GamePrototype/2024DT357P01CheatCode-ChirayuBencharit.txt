1. Change Scene:
   - Hold `L` + Press `1`: Load Level 1
   - Hold `L` + Press `2`: Load Level 2
   - Hold `L` + Press `3`: Load Level 3
   - Hold `L` + Press `4`: Load Level 4

2. Return to Main Menu:
   - Press `Escape`: Go back to the main menu.

3. Infinite Glider:
   - Press `G`: Unlock infinite glider usage.

4. Disable Enemies:
   - Press `K`: Disable all enemies in the scene.
